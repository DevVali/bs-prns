What are pronouns?

Pronouns are type of words that replace a noun.
For example, in the sentence "Carol likes football" we'll replace the noun (Carol) with pronoun "he", "He likes football".

What types of pronouns do we know?

There are several types of pronouns and one of them are prefferend pronouns.

What are prefferend pronouns used for?

They're used to describe a person.
You can chooose from a several prefferend pronouns. Some of them we can also talk as neo-pronouns.

What are neo-pronouns?

Neo-pronouns are a set of pronouns except the pronouns he/it/she/they and they're used to describe a person.
They're also chose to to avoid mistaking them as plurals, and to aid when language barriers are an issue.
